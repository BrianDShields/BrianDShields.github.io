Biography

Brian is an experienced entrepreneur and technology executive who has served in leadership roles throughout his career in banking, marketing, and strategy consulting. A recognized innovator with a strong business acumen developed over 12+ years working with companies ranging in size from Fortune 500 and Inc. 500 to early stage startups, Debt and capital markets, digital marketing and technology, venture capital, private equity, entrepreneurship. international capital markets, alternative investments in technology and media. emerging technologies, BlockChain, Virtual Reality, Automation: artificial intelligence and machine learning. product design, product development, product management, product marketing. talent discovery, mentorship, training, and professional development. Confident, high energy communicator capable of breaking down complex subjects for diverse audiences. Excellent written and oral communication skills. Agile, adaptable leader accustomed to multitasking across workstreams in a fast-paced work environment. Capable on working independently while efficiently managing multiple project teams. Brian speaks conversational French and German and his personal interests include travel, art, history, cinema, economics, and political affairs. His career began with a summer working at the local pool concession stand followed by summers as a runner on the floor of the Chicago Board of Trade and the Chicago Mercantile Exchange. Brian graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Finance from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a concentration in Cinema Studies. Brian has served as the Chief Executive Officer of Coder Inc. since 2015.
